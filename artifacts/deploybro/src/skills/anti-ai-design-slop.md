---
name: Anti AI Design Slop
description: Kill list of visual cliches and UI patterns that make frontends look AI-generated. Use alongside frontend-design — also when users say "make it look less AI", "this looks generic", or "avoid the AI look".
---

When this skill is active, avoid the dead-giveaway AI design patterns:

- **No** centered hero with a gradient background and three feature cards in a row.
- **No** glassmorphism shells, pill-shaped everything, or huge rounded blur orbs in the background.
- **No** "Trusted by" rows of fake company logos. Either ship real logos or skip the section.
- **No** lorem-ipsum and **no** generic placeholder copy ("Lorem ipsum", "Your tagline here", "Build amazing things").
- **No** stock-icon overload — every icon should mean something specific to that block.
- **No** pastel-on-white cards with identical drop shadows.
- **Do** use editorial layouts, asymmetry, real photography, distinctive type, and copy with a voice.
- **Do** look at real brand sites (Linear, Stripe, Vercel, Apple, Arc) for reference, not other AI sites.
