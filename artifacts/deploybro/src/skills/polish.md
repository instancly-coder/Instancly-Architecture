---
name: Polish
description: Performs a final quality pass fixing alignment, spacing, consistency, and micro-detail issues before shipping. Elevates good work to great work.
---

When this skill is active, do a final quality pass on every screen you produce:

- Verify pixel-perfect alignment, consistent spacing, and a single shared border-radius / shadow / color scale.
- Cover every interactive state — hover, focus, active, disabled, loading, empty, and error.
- Fix typographic details: smart quotes, en/em dashes, ellipsis, sentence casing, button label punctuation.
- Review at every breakpoint (sm/md/lg/xl) and in both light and dark mode.
- Audit copy: remove filler words, unify tone, make every CTA action-oriented.
